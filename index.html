What Makes HHKB So great
Dip switches are extremely useful for customization and make swapping between OS incredibly quick.
High quality plastic body and PBT keycaps stay dry and resist shine.
Topre switches are not only light, but very responsive allowing for less finger strain and a pleasurable typing experience.
Connect to multiple systems through bluetooth or USB with the flip of a [dip] switch.
